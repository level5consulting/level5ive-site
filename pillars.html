Capacity & Resource Planning

Amplifies

Tools People

Your people know where teams are stretched. Your tools hold the assignment data. AI turns both into a live, probabilistic model of real capacity — before overload materializes and causes damage.

AI Methods Deployed

Erlang Queuing Models

Bottleneck probabilities calculated under variable demand — mathematically, not by gut feel.

Utilization Variance Alerts

Alerts fire when utilization variance crosses a statistically significant threshold.

Without AI

Experienced PMs flag overload — after it's already causing pain

With Level 5ive AI

Queuing models predict team-level overload before it materializes

Risk & Predictive Delivery

Amplifies

Process People

Your processes include risk reviews. Your people assess status. AI replaces subjective RAG updates with a continuously calculated probability of on-time delivery — expressed as a number, not a color.

AI Methods Deployed

Monte Carlo Simulation

Runs daily across all delivery variables. Output: P(completion ≤ target) = 82%.

Survival Analysis

Identifies exact time-to-failure milestones — where projects are most likely to break down.

Without AI

Weekly RAG status updates based on PM judgment

With Level 5ive AI

Daily Monte Carlo outputs a true probability of on-time delivery

Statistical Process Control

UCL/LCL Threshold Alerts
