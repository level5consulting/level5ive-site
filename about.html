About Greg

Two decades inside
the enterprise.
Now fixing it.

Greg Stellflue is the Founder of Level 5ive Consulting and creator of the GO Framework — the methodology for governing and organizing AI that enterprises are missing. He has spent more than two decades working inside enterprise EPMOs, leading portfolio management functions, building governance frameworks from scratch, and sitting in the rooms where strategy gets written.

That means he knows exactly where the gap between AI intent and AI execution lives — because he's lived on both sides of it.

"Most EPMOs aren't failing because they lack tools or talent. They're failing because nothing is connecting those assets intelligently. That's the problem AI was built to solve."

— Greg Stellflue, Founder, Level 5ive Consulting
